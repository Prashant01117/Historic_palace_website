Explore India's Rich History

The Taj Mahal

A white marble mausoleum built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his third wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Agra Fort

A historical fort in Agra, served as the main residence of Mughal emperors until 1638.

Hampi

Ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, known for its temples, palaces, and bazaars.

Khajuraho Temples

Group of Hindu and Jain temples famous for their erotic sculptures and architectural style.
